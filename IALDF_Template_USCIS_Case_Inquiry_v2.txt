5.  Have you received any communications, requests for evidence (RFEs), or notices from USCIS? If yes, summarize what each one said.
 
 
 
 
6.  What is the most recent action or status update on your case?
 
 
  SECTION C — STATUS & AUTHORIZATION
7.  What is your current immigration status? (e.g., F-1, H-1B, AOS pending, LPR, etc.)
 
 
8.  Do you currently have work authorization? If yes, when does it expire?
 
 
9.  Have you ever overstayed a visa, worked without authorization, or had any prior immigration violations? Be specific.
 
 
 
  SECTION D — BACKGROUND & HISTORY
10.  Do you have any criminal history, arrests, or pending charges (in the U.S. or abroad)?
 
 
 
11.  Have you traveled outside the U.S. in the last 5 years? List dates and destinations.

  SECTION E — GOALS & CONCERNS
13.  What is your main concern or question about your case?
 
 
 
14.  What outcome are you hoping for? (Short term and long term.)
 
 
 
15.  Are there time-sensitive issues your attorney should know about? (Job loss, expiring documents, planned travel, family emergency.)
