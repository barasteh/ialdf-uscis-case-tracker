J-1 Exchange Visitor Status

J-1 status is governed by the dates on your DS-2019 form and your program sponsor’s approval. The pause affects some J-1 applications, but your underlying status is tied to your sponsor.

Your sponsor is your first call

Contact your program sponsor directly for guidance. They administer your DS-2019 and can confirm what is and is not affected for your specific category.

Program transfers

You cannot transfer a J-1 program to a different program category — transfers must remain within the same category. If you are considering a program change that involves a different J-1 category, this would require a new application and would be subject to the pause.13

What to ask your sponsor
  • Is my DS-2019 still in good standing? When does it expire?
  • Do I need any USCIS-administered benefit (such as a category change) that would be subject to the pause?
  • What does the 30-day grace period after my program end date look like for my situation?
